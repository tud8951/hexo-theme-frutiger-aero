Frutiger Aero Aesthetic

2026-05-28

Welcome to the Frutiger Aero era!

Frutiger Aero is an aesthetic that was prevalent in the mid-2000s to early 2010s. It is characterized by:

  • Glossy textures: Everything looks like it’s made of glass or shiny plastic.
  • Nature and Technology: Images of green grass, blue skies, and water droplets combined with sleek gadgets.
  • Skeuomorphism: UI elements that mimic real-world objects.
  • Vibrant Colors: Lots of bright blues, greens, and whites.

How to achieve this style?

In this theme, we use:

  1. backdrop-filter for the glass effect.
  2. Linear and radial gradients for the glossy feel.
  3. Rounded corners everywhere!

Enjoy the nostalgia!
